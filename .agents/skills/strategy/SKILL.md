# Strategy

Choose better product bets before overbuilding. Convert research into decisions, experiments, and sharper product scope.

## Start

1. Read `PRODUCT.md` and `CONTEXT.md` when present.
2. Identify the decision being made.
3. Separate facts, assumptions, guesses, and recommendations.
4. If the task depends on current product, customer, revenue, acquisition, or connected-account evidence, read `../building-like-bytedance/references/data-acquisition.md`.
5. Prefer current product evidence over generic frameworks. Use AnyHost evidence when bound, and equivalent provider/user-provided evidence when not bound.

## Workflows

- Market or audience research: read `references/market-research.md`.
- PMF, wedge, STP, positioning, or 4P: read `references/positioning.md`.
- Roadmap, sequencing, product bets, ROI tradeoffs, or operating strategy: read `references/prioritization.md`.
- Durable product decision: read `.agents/specs/artifacts.md` and write or update a strategy artifact when the work changes positioning, pricing, roadmap, target user, wedge, or validation plan.

## Strategy Output

Use this shape:

```markdown
## Decision

## Evidence

## Assumptions

## Options

## Recommendation

## Next Validation
```

Keep recommendations tied to a concrete product move: feature, experiment, message, segment, pricing test, or research question.
