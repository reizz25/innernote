# Acquisition Funnel

Use when diagnosing where paid or organic growth breaks.

## Funnel

Default paid acquisition chain:

```text
impression -> click -> landing page view -> signup/lead -> activation -> paid conversion -> retention -> expansion/referral
```

Do not stop at the ad-platform conversion if the product goal depends on activation, revenue, or retention.

## Bottlenecks

| Symptom | Likely bottleneck |
| --- | --- |
| High spend, low impressions | bidding, audience size, budget pacing, policy/review issue |
| Impressions, low CTR | creative, positioning, audience mismatch |
| Clicks, low landing-page view | tracking, load speed, redirect, low-quality clicks |
| Landing views, low signup/lead | offer, page clarity, trust, form friction |
| Signup, low activation | onboarding, product fit, setup friction |
| Activation, low paid conversion | pricing, packaging, sales follow-up, value proof |
| Paid conversion, low retention | wrong audience, expectation mismatch, product quality |
