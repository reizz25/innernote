# Channel Cohort Analysis

Use when comparing acquisition quality across channels, campaigns, creatives, keywords, geographies, personas, or landing pages.

## Cohort Table

```markdown
| Cohort | Channel | Campaign | Spend | New users | Activated users | Paying users | D7 retention | D30 retention | Revenue | Contribution LTV | CAC | Payback | ROI |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
```

## Segment Cuts

Use the cuts that match the decision:

- channel
- campaign
- ad set/ad group
- creative/ad
- keyword/search term
- landing page
- geography
- device/platform
- plan/package
- persona or company segment
- cohort date/week/month

## Diagnosis

Look for:

- high-CAC but high-LTV cohorts worth scaling
- low-CAC cohorts with poor activation/retention
- campaigns with delayed payback but strong retention
- creative/keyword mismatch that drives clicks but not activation
- landing pages that convert signups but not qualified users
- organic cohorts that outperform paid cohorts and should inform paid messaging

Never average away cohort age. A D7 cohort and D90 cohort are not comparable without modeled or normalized LTV.
